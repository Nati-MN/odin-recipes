Whey Protein Shake - Recipe

This chocolate protein shake is high in protein and tasty


Ingredients

  • 6 ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop chocolate-flavored whey protein powder
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder, or more to taste


Directions

  1. Blend ice cubes, milk, banana, protein powder, peanut butter, honey, and cocoa powder together in a blender until smooth.

    2. Enjoy your protein shake and remember - "THE BODY ACHIEVES WHAT THE MIND BELIEVES"
